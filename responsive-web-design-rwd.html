What is Responsive Web Design?

A Responsive Website Design (RWD) is a site that will respond to any device when viewed, be it smart phone, tablet or computer. Search engines give a boost in their ranking to the responsible website. So it is important for your business that your website should be responsible and user-friendly.

Responsive Web Design is about using CSS and HTML to resize, hide, shrink, enlarge, or move the content to make it look good on any screen. Single website that adjusts/adapts to multiple devices. Responsive Design VS Mobile-First Design

These concepts can sometimes lead to confusion; some people think they are one and the same when they are entirely different. Responsive Design starts with the desktop. You start at the maximum required resolution and then scale down to the smallest screen. Although the content and layout will adjust to fit smartphones and tablets, the navigation, content and download speeds are geared more for your traditional website.
